Your rotations will include:

- **Paediatric Operating Theatres**  
- **PICU & NICU**  
- **Simulation Workshops**  
- **Elective Blocks**  

{{< callout emoji="⏰" title="Block Lengths" >}}
Most rotations run for **4 weeks**; some critical care blocks may extend to **6 weeks**.
